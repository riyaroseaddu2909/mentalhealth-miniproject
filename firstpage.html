Emotions we feel

Happy

Feelings: Joy, contentment, excitement

Reasons: Achievements, connection, gratitude

When: Celebrations, quality time, good news

Sad

Feelings: Hopelessness, heaviness, withdrawal

Reasons: Loss, loneliness, unmet expectations

When: After rejection, missing someone

Anger

Feelings: Frustration, irritation, resentment

Reasons: Injustice, conflict, lack of control

When: Arguments, stress overload, delays

Grief

Feelings: Deep sorrow, emptiness, yearning

Reasons: Loss of a loved one, separation

When: Bereavement, breakups, major changes

Anxiety

Feelings: Uneasiness, restlessness, worry

Reasons: Uncertainty, overthinking, fear

When: Exams, deadlines, unknown outcomes

Confused

Feelings: Uncertainty, hesitation, doubt

Reasons: Difficult decisions, lack of clarity

When: Career choices, relationships, new paths

Calm

Feelings: Peaceful, balanced, composed

Reasons: Meditation, nature, self-care

When: After rest, mindfulness practice

Hopeful

Feelings: Optimism, motivation, determination

Reasons: New opportunities, support, progress

When: Starting projects, receiving encouragement
